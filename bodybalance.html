What is neuromuscular therapy?

Treating the cause of your pain — by hand.

Neuromuscular therapy is a specialised, hands-on treatment that works on your muscles and soft tissue — releasing tight areas, easing trigger points and taking the strain off the nerves and joints that are causing you pain.

Every session is built around you: your history, your injury and your goals. The aim is simple — relieve your pain, restore your movement, and help your body recover properly so it lasts.

  • 01Hands-on therapy.Skilled, targeted techniques tailored to you.
  • 02Whole-body care.We look at movement, function and lifestyle.
  • 03Results-driven.Real progress, real improvements.
