Questions

Before you ask

Do I need to be fit before I start? +

No. Every service scales to where you are today. The Club takes complete beginners every Saturday, and First Miles exists precisely for the person who's never run further than the end of the road.

What does the Run Club cost? +

Nothing. It's free and it stays free — no trial period, no catch, no upsell at the finish line. Just turn up.

Is coaching online or in person? +

Both. Online Coaching works wherever you are in the world. Personal training and Strength to Run happen in person in Limerick. Race Ready can run either way — plenty of athletes prep remotely and see us at the Club on Saturdays.

I have an event booked — how much lead time do you need? +

More is always better. For a half marathon or longer, twelve weeks or more is ideal. But message us either way — we'll tell you honestly what's achievable in the time you've got, and what isn't.

Can I switch between services? +

Yes. Coaching is monthly with no long lock-in, and plenty of people start with the free Club, move into coaching for an event, then settle back into Club life after. Start where you are; step up when you're ready.

Which one should I pick? +

If in doubt: come to the Club on Saturday. It costs nothing and you'll meet us before you commit to anything. Or send a message and we'll point you at the right service — even when that's not the most expensive one.
